Wait, Who is Nick Kury?

"Question Everything"
That's the approach that I take in all aspects of my life. I want to know how and why things work.
I've use this framework in my time studying history, to my current passion of software development, and even my hobbies like building computers and videogames. I seek understanding and after that improvement. I believe that we as people should grow and understand the world around us. When I am not spending my time in front of a screen you can find me cooking, at a concert or chilling at the beach. Feel free to reach out to talk about software development, some of my other hobbies, or just to get to know one another.
